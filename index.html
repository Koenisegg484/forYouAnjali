When I grow old, my pyaar for you will remain the same,

Sending you messages from my desk,

In the soft glow of the lamp, with the rain outside,

Taking half a day to say, "I miss you,"

In the fields of my heart,

Sometimes stars chase the moon, sometimes wild horses run.


Sometimes under the moonlight,

I dream a winding dream,

Every turn filled with you,

Your smile lighting up my day.
